Vacuum Metalizing Processs

Metalizing is a technology that can apply metal on almost any kind of surface.

Metal can be aluminum, chrome, gold...

It can apply almost any kind of material like plastic, metal, ceramic, glass...

Most part of metalizing apply on plastic, unlike traditional electroplating chrome plating only can apply on abs/pc, our metalizing can apply almost any kind of plastic, abs, pbt, pvc, pe, pc, ps, pmma, nylon...

Vacuum Metalizing also known as PVD (Physical Vapor Deposition), which can attach metal film.

Vacuum Metalizing can create many shinny color!
